Phase 1: Choose a song you would prefer to sing.

Listen to a short excerpt from a pair of songs. You will be asked which of these songs you would prefer to sing as well as your familiarity and preference for them.

  • Please listen to each sample all the way through before making your decision
  • You can listen to each sample only twice before making your decision
  • Try not to overthink and make your choice as quickly as possible
  • Some excerpts may have cursing
